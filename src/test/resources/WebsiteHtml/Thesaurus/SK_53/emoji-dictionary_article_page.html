‍ Heart on Fire emoji

[ hahrt on fahyuhr ih-moh-jee ]

What does ‍ Heart on Fire emoji mean?

The Heart on Fire emoji ‍ depicts a flaming heart. It can be used to represent passionate love (“burning love”) or intense liking of something.

Break out the Bottle with Popping Cork emoji  and Confetti Ball emoji for the party!  July 17 is World Emoji Day.

Where does ‍ Heart on Fire emoji come from?

The Heart on Fire emoji ‍ was added to Emoji 13.1 in 2020. On supported platforms, the emoji is created by a combination of the Red Heart emoji and Fire emoji .

As of July 2021, Google, Apple, and Twitter devices support the emoji. The emoji resembles a stylized red heart sitting in orange or reddish-orange flames. On unsupported platforms, the Heart on Fire emoji ‍ will appear as the Red Heart emoji followed by the Fire emoji .
