cus·tom·ize

 (kŭs′tə-mīz′)
tr.v. cus·tom·ized, cus·tom·iz·ing, cus·tom·iz·es
To make or alter to individual or personal specifications: customize a van.
cus′tom·i·za′tion (-ĭ-zā′shən) n.
cus′tom·iz′er n.

cus•tom•ize

(ˈkʌs təˌmaɪz)

v.t. -ized, -iz•ing.
to modify, make, or build according to individual specifications or preference.
